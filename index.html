About

Kolton is a Computer Science student at the University of Missouri. His research interests include computer vision and autonomous robotics with a particular emphasis on autonomous vehicles. He is the Head Electrical Engineer for the hydrogen car division of Mizzou Eco Racing .

TigerDrive

TigerDrive is the University of Missouri's first autonomous vehicle build project. It is entirely student led, developed, and maintained. The vehicle is a 1997 Chevy S-10 that has been converted to an electric vehicle by Mizzou Eco Racing. Kolton is making the vehicle autonomous as his senior capstone project. A brief presentation that details the project so far can be found here. The project is slated to be finished by May 2019.

Slither Bot

This project is a bot for the popular online game Slither.io. The bot uses a convolutional nerual network to make navigational choices in a multi-agent environment. Kolton is currently working on setting up the neccessary infrastructure to try and learn the game with reinforcment learning.
