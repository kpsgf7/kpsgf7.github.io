About

Kolton is a Computer Science student at the University of Missouri. His research interests include computer vision and autonomous robotics with a particular emphasis on autonomous vehicles. He is the Head Electrical Engineer for the hydrogen car division of Mizzou Eco Racing .

TigerDrive

TigerDrive is the University of Missouri's first autonomous vehicle build project. It is entirely student led, developed, and maintained. The vehicle is a 1997 Chevy S-10 that has been converted to an electric vehicle by Mizzou Eco Racing.
